Our Story

Founded in 2023, StaySync emerged from the frustration of dealing with clunky, outdated hotel management software. Our founders, a mix of seasoned hoteliers and software engineers, set out to build a platform that is as powerful as enterprise solutions but as easy to use as your favorite consumer apps.

Today, we serve hundreds of properties globally, helping them save hours on manual tasks, increase revenue, and deliver unforgettable guest experiences.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To simplify property management through intuitive design and robust automation, allowing hoteliers to focus on hospitality, not administration.

Vision

To become the global standard operating system for independent hotels, hostels, and vacation rentals worldwide.

Platform Modules

A comprehensive suite of tools designed to handle every aspect of your hospitality business.

Property Management System

The core of your operations. Drag-and-drop calendar, quick check-in/out, invoicing, and housekeeping coordination.

  • Interactive Tape Chart
  • Automated Night Audit
  • Custom Folios

Channel Manager

Sync your availability and rates across 200+ OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia, Airbnb) in real-time to prevent double bookings.

  • 2-Way XML Integration
  • Yield Management Rules
  • Centralized Inventory

Direct Booking Engine

Turn your website into your most profitable channel. Customizable, secure, and mobile-friendly booking widget.

  • Promo Codes & Upsells
  • Multi-currency / Language
  • Payment Gateway Integrations

Housekeeping App

Keep your rooms spotless and teams coordinated. Mobile view for maids to update room status instantly.

Integrated Payments

Process credit cards securely, automate deposits, and handle pre-authorizations seamlessly within the PMS.

Open API & Integrations

Connect to your favorite accounting tools, point-of-sale systems, and smart locks via our robust API.
